1928-1933 are the golden years of animation, yet most people do not know about the things happened during this era that contributed to the fantastic animations and characters we have today. This timeline website is dedicated to bring some insights into the milestones of animation made during the golden era. Its simple and friendly interface allows children and adults alike to understand and remember the timeline easily. Most of all, there's a quiz at the end of the reading for readers to play with!
